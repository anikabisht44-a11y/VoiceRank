1. DESIGN PHILOSOPHY
Principle	How It Shows
Confidence	Bold typography, clean white space, strong CTAs
Authority	Score meters feel scientific, not gimmicky
Simplicity	4 screens, no clutter, one primary action per screen
Community	Rank score cards are shareable — users become advocates
Inspiration from Storyteller:

Clean editorial grid

"Story of the Week" becomes "Blog of the Week" leaderboard

Community-first language: "Join the VoiceRank community" instead of "Sign up"
